Services

Website Design & Hosting

We build beautiful, clean and fast-loading websites using powerful HTML5|CSS3 frameworks or Wordpress. Our commitment to responsive design means your website will look great on any device or screen. Need domain registration and web hosting? No problem, we'll handle that for you as well.

Graphic Design

Every design agency says they are unique, but Red Studio's creative philosophy is special and is reflected in the work we create. Whether it's a new logo, web graphics or your dream wedding invitations, we communicate your message with vibrant artwork, bold typography & colour and custom-crafted graphic elements.

Branding & Identity

We'll design and layout your business stationery (ie: letterheads, business cards) and your marketing material (brochures, print advertisements, magazines). Need to have your work printed? Whether local or overseas, we can handle the details with the printery so you can get on with running your business.

Social Media Management

Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, TripAdvisor, blogging. Today, social media is a huge part of your marketing strategy. You have got to be present online interacting with your customers and driving your sales. We'll set up your social media accounts, advise and assist with engagement and even handle the daily management of your online presence.

Do you have an idea for an app for your business? Come talk to us. We'll connect you with some of the best local and regional app developers.

About

Hi, I'm Raquel from RED Studio, a small, web & graphic design agency. I LOVE great design and convey that passion in the work I create.
