Helpful Hints

To obtain a local driver's permit visitors must present the following to the Royal Grenada Police Force - Traffic Dept on The Carenage:

  • National driver's license
  • Passport
  • Counterpart license for persons coming from the UK only.

The local driver's permit is valid for three months and costs US$40.00.

Driving in Grenada is similar to the English system, where driving is on the LEFT.

A mandatory 15% Value Added Tax (VAT) is added to your final bill.
