Web Design

RED Studio builds clean, attractive and fast-loading websites using powerful HTML5|CSS3 frameworks as a base. This ensures your website is responsive and will look great when viewed on devices such as tablets and smartphones. Domain name registration, site hosting and on-going support and updates can be arranged. SEO (or search engine optimization) involves making sure your site ranks high in searches and we can work with you on keyword and outreach strategy to expand your visibility on the web.

Graphic Design

We work hand in hand with you to create custom logos and artwork, portraying the essence of your business and philosophy. RED Studio also create wedding and event invitations, website graphics, artwork for flyers, signs and banners. We can arrange printing of your material and advertisement placement in industry publications if you wish. RED is a one-stop shop for your marketing and promotional needs.

Branding & Identity

RED Studio handles the pre-press and offset preparation and layout of your business stationery (letterheads, business cards, envelopes) and a full-range of marketing material (brochures, coupons and flyers). Need to have your work printed? No problem. Whether local or overseas, we can make the necessary arrangements with the printery and handle the details so that you can get on with running your business.

Social Media Management

Let's be blunt: in today's environment, social media is the vital tool in your company's marketing arsenal. You need to be accessable and interactive on Facebook, Twitter and Google+, and maybe even LinkedIn or Etsy. Let us guide you through the often confusing world of social media, ensuring you are efficiently targeting your market and effectively delivering your message. We can even handle the day-to-day management of your online presence, posting updates and press releases, responding to and monitoring your customer engagement.
